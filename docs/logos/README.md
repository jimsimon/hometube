# HomeTube Logo Concepts

Five SVG concepts, all 200×200 viewBox, scalable to any size, suitable
for favicons, app icons, and the header. All use a friendly, rounded
geometry intended to feel safe and kid-appropriate while still nodding
to YouTube's video-player heritage (the red play triangle).

| # | File | Concept | Palette |
|---|------|---------|---------|
| 1 | [`01-house-play.svg`](./01-house-play.svg) | **House = Screen.** A red house whose front facade _is_ the video screen, with the YouTube-style play triangle inside. Most literal interpretation of the name. | YouTube red + warm coral |
| 2 | [`02-shield-play.svg`](./02-shield-play.svg) | **Safety Shield.** A blue shield containing a little house silhouette with a play triangle. Leans into the parental-control / safe-for-kids angle. | Trust blue + red accent |
| 3 | [`03-tv-antenna.svg`](./03-tv-antenna.svg) | **Retro TV House.** A vintage TV set whose rabbit-ear antennae form a roof peak. Playful, nostalgic, distinctive. | Warm amber + brown |
| 4 | [`04-monogram-ht.svg`](./04-monogram-ht.svg) | **HT Monogram.** An "H" whose roof and crossbar play-button double as a house. Works great as a square app icon / favicon. | Indigo gradient + yellow roof |
| 5 | [`05-pillow-play.svg`](./05-pillow-play.svg) | **Cozy Pillow.** A soft squircle "pillow" with a roof peak and a big play triangle. Most minimal — reads well at 16×16. | Teal + cream |

## House-Play Variations (trademark-safe)

Variations on concept #1 that deliberately avoid YouTube's protected trade
dress — specifically the **red rounded-rectangle badge containing a white
play triangle**. These swap the colour, the badge shape, or the inner
glyph entirely.

| # | File | What's different |
|---|------|------------------|
| 1a | [`01a-house-blue-circle.svg`](./01a-house-blue-circle.svg) | Blue palette; play sits inside a **circle**, not a rounded rectangle. |
| 1b | [`01b-house-green-arrow.svg`](./01b-house-green-arrow.svg) | Green/brown palette; a softened, rounded-corner arrow over an arched door — reads as "play" without mimicking the YT badge. |
| 1c | [`01c-house-purple-pictogram.svg`](./01c-house-purple-pictogram.svg) | Purple palette; the screen shows a **parent-and-child pictogram** instead of a play triangle. Emphasises the family/kids angle. |
| 1d | [`01d-house-teal-outline.svg`](./01d-house-teal-outline.svg) | Teal **line-art** house with a soft pentagon play glyph — very distinct silhouette. |
| 1e | [`01e-house-sunset-window.svg`](./01e-house-sunset-window.svg) | Orange/brown palette; the front is clearly a **window with a cross-frame**, with a small circular play badge in one pane. |

> These are concept sketches, not legal advice. If branding goes public,
> have a trademark attorney review the final mark against YouTube/Google's
> registered marks in your jurisdiction.

## Recommendation

- **Primary brand mark:** `01-house-play.svg` — clearest read of the name
  and matches the existing red accent used in the app.
- **App icon / favicon:** `04-monogram-ht.svg` — strongest silhouette at
  small sizes.
- **Marketing / "safe for kids" hero:** `02-shield-play.svg`.
